1. Introduction

Welcome to VoiceToPost ("we," "our," or "us"). We are a free, non-commercial demonstration project that uses artificial intelligence and voice commands to research topics, generate social media posts, and schedule content to your connected social media accounts.

This is a student/IEEE presentation project. The Service is provided free of charge and is not intended for commercial use. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our website, web application, and related services (collectively, the "Service").

By using our Service, you consent to the data practices described in this policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our Service.

2.2 Voice Input Data

  • Your voice input is transmitted only while microphone is active.
  • Audio is transcribed via Web Speech API or secure transcription service.
  • We do not store voice recordings – only transcribed text is saved.
  • Transcribed text is treated with the same confidentiality as typed text.
  • Voice commands are optional; text input is always available.

2.3 Social Media Account Information

We request only minimum permissions necessary. We never access DMs, private messages, or passwords.

4.1 Twitter/X

  • OAuth 2.0 PKCE
  • Permanent authorization unless revoked
  • Only tweet.write and tweet.read scopes

4.2 LinkedIn (60-Day Expiry)

  • r_liteprofile + w_member_social scopes
  • Manual reconnect required every 60 days

5. Artificial Intelligence and Agentic RAG

We use Gemini 3 Pro and haiku.rag for AI generation and research.

  • Autonomous research loops (max 3 iterations)
  • Confidence scoring system
  • Clickable citations
  • Platform-optimized post generation

You retain full ownership of generated content. You are responsible for reviewing before publishing.
